Our Recycling Process

A systematic 5-step process to transform plastic waste into valuable resources

1

Plastic Collection

We collect plastic waste from various sources including households, industries, and commercial establishments through our extensive network.

2

Sorting

Advanced sorting technology separates plastics by type, color, and quality to ensure optimal recycling outcomes.

3

Cleaning

Thorough washing and decontamination process removes impurities, labels, and adhesives using eco-friendly methods.

4

Melting

Cleaned plastic is melted at controlled temperatures and transformed into molten form ready for reshaping.

5

Product Making

The recycled plastic is molded into new products including bottles, sheets, bags, and other useful items.
